My Special Sauce Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2-3 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon ranch
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon hot sauce

Instructions

In a bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, ketchup, ranch. It should be a light/pale orange color.

If you want to add a little spice, mix in your hot sauce.

Add and mix the Worcestershire sauce, then add the pepper and mix again.

Enjoy Your Sauce!

This sauce is perfect for burgers, sandwiches, or as a dip for your favorite snacks!
